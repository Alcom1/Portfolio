TEAM TYRO

Role: Preproduction, Programmer

Framework: XNA

Team Tyro, originally called "Chaos University", is a Stealth Puzzle game with tile based movement. Players guide their characters from level to level by placing, turning, and removing directional tiles, with a limit to how many tiles they can place. Players must make clever use of tiles along with special character abilities to get past guards, collect intel papers, and proceed through an assortment of levels.
