HEXGRID HEROES

Role: Character conceptualizing, asset development.

Hexgrid Heroes is a turn-based asymmetrical board game that involves knocking opposing players off a hexagon grid arena. Each player choses among four unique heroes, and uses their active and passive abilities to deal damage to opponents, increase their opponents damage tier, and use their opponent's damaged state to force them off the map. Hexgrid Heroes was heavily play-tested and tweaked to better ensure a reasonable level of asymmetrical balance.
