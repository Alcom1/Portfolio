AUTONOMOUS SPACE COMBAT SIMULATION

Press 1-9 to switch cameras

Role: All

Engine: Unity

A simulation that involves a formation of bombers attacking a frigate in space. This simulation uses a variety of advanced steering behaviors to guide both bombers and missiles, including special varieties of evade and persuit that perfectly predict their target's future position, and flocking to ensure the bombers fly in formation.
