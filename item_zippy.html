PROJECT ZIPPY

Role: Player Controls and Physics development. Collision detection, resolution, and optimization development.

Engine: ReEngine

Project Zippy is a project to develop first person shooter controls in the OpenGL ReEngine. Project Zippy utlizes several advanced techniques to produce smooth controls and an effective experience, including Arvo's algorithm for collision detection between the player sphere and the environment, and a per-frame updating octree for collision optimization.
