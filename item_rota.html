ROTA

Controls

Place and rotate left: left-mouse click

Place and rotate right: right-mouse click

Role: All

Language: Processing

Rota is a variety of Connect-4 featuring a board that rotates at right angles between turns. The game is complete with physics, win-state detection, and a purely random AI opponent for single-player.
